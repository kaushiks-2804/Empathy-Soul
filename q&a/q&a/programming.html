What is Blockchain Technology?

Asked by: Zainab Qureshi

Answer: Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger that records transactions across many computers. It ensures transparency and security by using cryptographic techniques.

What is Cloud Computing?

Asked by: Ahmed Ali

Answer: Cloud computing allows users to access computing resources over the internet, providing scalable and on-demand services such as storage, databases, and computing power.

What are the Benefits of 5G Technology?

Asked by: Omar Khan

Answer: 5G technology offers faster internet speeds, lower latency, and greater network reliability. It enables enhanced mobile experiences and supports the growth of IoT (Internet of Things) devices.

What is Quantum Computing?

Asked by: Faizan Shah

Answer: Quantum computing leverages quantum mechanics to perform complex computations much faster than classical computers, with potential applications in cryptography and optimization problems.
